## Key Projects Mentioned

### SEMCL.ONE
A community-driven Software Composition Analysis platform combining open-source tools, shared infrastructure, and AI automation for compliance management.
- URL: https://community.semcl.one
- Tools: PURL2SRC, SRC2PURL, OSSLILI, Semantic CopyCat, MCP-SEMCLONE, OSPAC

### BinarySniffer
An open-source security scanner for AI models (pickle files, PyTorch, ONNX, SafeTensors) that detects malicious payloads without executing them.
